Border Empires

Privacy Policy

Your empire, your data.

Effective date: March 20, 2026

This draft is a practical product-ready starting point for Border Empires, but it should still be reviewed against your final support process, analytics setup, and jurisdictional requirements before launch.

1. What this policy covers

This Privacy Policy explains how Border Empires collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you create an account, sign in, and play the game across devices.

2. Information we collect

  • Account information such as your email address, authentication provider, display name, and Firebase account identifier.
  • Gameplay information such as territory ownership, progression, resources, alliances, messages, combat outcomes, and other in-game state tied to your account.
  • Technical information such as browser type, device type, IP address, coarse location inferred from network data, and diagnostic logs necessary to operate, secure, and improve the game.
  • Support information you choose to send if you contact us with bug reports or account issues.

3. How we use your information

  • To authenticate you and keep your empire persistent across sessions and devices.
  • To run the live game, process actions, store progression, and synchronize multiplayer state.
  • To protect the game from abuse, fraud, cheating, service disruption, and account misuse.
  • To troubleshoot bugs, monitor performance, and improve balance, reliability, and usability.
  • To respond to support requests and communicate important service notices.

4. Authentication and third-party services

Border Empires currently uses Firebase Authentication to support sign-in methods such as Google sign-in, email/password sign-in, and passwordless email link sign-in. When you use those methods, the relevant provider and Firebase may process your login information according to their own policies.

5. How long we keep data

We keep account and gameplay data for as long as needed to operate the game, maintain account continuity, resolve disputes, enforce our rules, and meet legal obligations. We may delete or anonymize certain data when it is no longer needed for those purposes.

6. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with service providers that help us operate the game, such as hosting, authentication, storage, analytics, and support tools. We may also disclose information if required by law or if reasonably necessary to protect our users, services, or legal rights.

7. In-game visibility

Certain information is visible to other players as part of normal gameplay, including your display name, territory, alliances, combat outcomes, leaderboard placement, and other game-facing state. This visibility is part of the core multiplayer experience.

8. Your choices

  • You may choose which sign-in method to use where available.
  • You may stop playing at any time, though some account data may still be retained as described above.
  • You can request account-related help or deletion review through the support channel that distributes Border Empires.

9. Children’s privacy

Border Empires is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information without appropriate consent, contact us so we can investigate and take appropriate action.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we may provide notice in the game, through the website, or by other reasonable means.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, use the official Border Empires support channel or replace this section with your public support email before launch.
